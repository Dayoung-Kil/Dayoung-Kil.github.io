👋 Introduction

I am Dayoung, a Ph.D. candidate at the Graduate School of Soongsil University, pursuing my research under the guidance of Professor Seong Heum Kim in the Visual Intelligence and Platform (VIP) Lab. My academic journey is dedicated to pushing the boundaries of hardware-efficient artificial intelligence, with a keen focus on bridging cutting-edge deep learning methodologies and real-world deployment efficiency.

By combining algorithmic innovation with system-level optimization, I aim to develop AI models that are not only powerful but also sustainable, scalable, and deployable in resource-constrained environments. My work spans from designing efficient deep learning architectures to pioneering advanced pruning techniques for large-scale multimodal systems.

Research Interests

  • Computer Vision
  • Large Vision-Language Models (LVLMs)
  • Multimodal Large Language Models (MM-LLMs)
  • Efficient Deep Learning
  • Model Pruning
